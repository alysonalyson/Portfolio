ABOUT ME

I am a Front-End Web Developer based in Austin, TX, working with HTML/CSS/JS and React to create eye-catching and functional websites and web apps. My design and development philosophy is guided by efficiency, intuition, and the user experience, with the goal of creating positive digital space that is accessible to everyone.

When I'm not in front of a computer, I love to get outside to hike, swim, or run (when my hamstring isn't acting up, anyway), and love sports (especially hockey and soccer). I've worked with kids for over 10 years as a teacher, nanny, or soccer coach, and hope to soon have a few kid-oriented development projects to share.
